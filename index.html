Mel Bochner s

I would like to note Mel Bochners passing on February 12, 2025. He was a valued colleague, a brilliant interlocutor, never brought less than his A game to studio visits and reviews, he had a voice that brought insight to the conversation, and had an outstanding sense of humor. I was fortunate to be his colleague. Students felt fortunate for the exchange they shared. He never varnished his truth. May he rest in peace. —Rochelle Feinstein, Emerita Professor, School of Art, 1994-2017
